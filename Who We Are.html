Who We Are

Alpha Kappa Psi is a Co-ed Professional Business Fraternity recognized for developing the worlds premier business leaders. We prepare brothers for a successful filled life after college by preparing them professionally and connecting them with our broad network of companies ranging from Amazon, IBM, Goldman Sachs, and many more.

Our Core Values

Our brothers live by 5 core values to prepare to become premier business leaders: Brotherhood, Unity, Intergrity, Knowledge, and Service.
