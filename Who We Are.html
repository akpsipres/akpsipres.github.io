Who Are We

Alpha Kappa Psi is the premier developer of business leaders. We are a Co-Ed Professional Business Fraternity open to individuals of all majors. Our mission is to develop your professional side through focusing on our 5 core values in which consist of Brotherhood, Unity, Integrity, Knowledge, and Service.

Our Core Values

Our core values consist of: Brotherhood forges lifelong bonds that last beyond the workplace, because growing your relationships opens doors for your future career. Unity indicates that brothers stand together as we overcome obstacles so we can continue to spread our mission into leadership across the country. Integrity is stressed in every aspect of our organization because premier professionals instill moral and ethical standards amongst their peers. Knowledge inspires our brothers to persevere as the quest for success shapens our minds. Service teaches our brothers humility and compassion as one should never forget the community they came from.

Join The Brotherhood

We have chapters across the world stretching to over 7 other continents, and each of those chapters consist of individuals from different races, cultures, backgrounds, and so much more. Our differences help us develop the next premier business leaders. Do you want to be apart of this journey?
